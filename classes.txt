Class Chair

Attributes:
floor_interface (string) ("casters", "feet", "rockers")
material (string) ("wood", "leather", "cloth fabric", "mesh")
seat_height (integer)
recline (boolean)
has_armrests (boolean)
creaky (boolean)

Methods:
adjust_height (change seat_height)
recline_extend (change recline to true)
recline_retract (change recline to false)
move_chair (change chair location)
